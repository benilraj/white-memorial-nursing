White Mission Hosptial
Attoor, Yettacode, Thiruvattar Taluk, Veeyanoor PO, Kanyakumari District.
Our White Mission hospital has the medical facility where patients receive diagnosis, treatment, and care for various illnesses, injuries, and medical conditions. Our hospital have a range of medical professionals, including doctors, nurses, and specialists, as well as advanced medical equipment and technology to provide comprehensive medical services. We offer emergency care, surgical procedures, diagnostic testing, and long-term care for patients with chronic conditions.
