What we Provide
LIBRARY
Separate Libraries are available for all colleges. The libraries have one of the largest collections of Medical books.Libraries serve as repositories of knowledge and culture, providing access to a vast array of books, journals, multimedia resources, and digital archives.
CLINICAL FACILITY
The WHITE MEMORIAL Hospital is a well-known multi-specialty hospital in the city of Comatose. It is a 300 bedded hospital with 26 specialty departments offering expert care.The students gain clinical experience through block postings at the various departments of WHITE MEMORIAL Hospitals The students also gain clinical experience from affiliated institutions, which are specialized hospitals providing clinical experience in specialty areas. They are: Institute of Mental Health, Kilpauk, Chennai for Mental Health Nursing and Coimbatore Corporation Maternity centres for Maternity Nursing experience.
HOSTELS
Separate Hostel facilities available for both Boys & Girls, well clean and hygienic Shared and Single room with attached restroom Breakfast, Lunch and Breakfast will be Provided.
TRANSPORTS
College Buses are available for students and staff. Day scholar Students can avail these Facilities.College buses provide convenient transportation for students, faculty, and staff between various locations such as campus, dormitories, parking lots, and nearby off-campus facilities.
SPORTS & RECREATION
The college provides sports and recreation facilities for the students through the department of Physical education. There are two Physical Directors (One male and one female) available to give coaching and training for the students.The sports facilities includes outdoor games like Volleyball, Basket Ball, Hand Ball, Tennikoit, Tennis, Shuttle and Ball Badminton and indoor games like Table Tennis, Carrom and Chess.There is also a large play ground for athletic and field events like running, shot put, Javelin and Discuss throw.
